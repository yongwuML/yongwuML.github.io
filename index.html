About Me

I am working as a postdoc in the Robotics & Embodied AI Lab at Tongji University. I got my Ph.D degree from Shanghai University in 2022. During my Ph.D period, I was supervised by Prof. Zhi Liu. In 2021.07-2022.07, I studied at University of Manitoba supervised by Prof. Yang Wang as a visiting Ph.D student. I received my MSc from Shantou University in 2018, where I was supervised by Prof. Zemin Cai. Now, my mentor is Prof. Guang Chen, and our lab is also supervised by Prof. Changjun Jiang. My research interestes lie in Machine Learning, Meta Learning, Domain Adaptation, and some applications e.g., Image Classfication, Gaze Estimation, Point Cloud, Saliency Detection, etc.
