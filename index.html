I am working as a postdoc in the Intelligent Perception and Computing Lab at Tongji University. I got my Ph.D degree from Shanghai University in 2022. During my Ph.D period, I was supervised by Prof. Zhi Liu. In 2021.07-2022.07, I studied at University of Manitoba supervised by Prof. Yang Wang as a visiting Ph.D student. I received my MSc from Shantou University in 2018, where I was supervised by Prof. Zemin Cai. Now, my partner mentor is Prof. Guang Chen. My research interestes lie in Meta Learning, Domain Adaptation, and Gaze Estimation.
